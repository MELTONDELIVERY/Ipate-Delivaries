Getting Started

Welcome to IPATE Deliveries! Here's how to get started with our platform:

  • Create an account by clicking "Join Now"
  • Verify your phone number and email address
  • Set up your delivery preferences and addresses
  • Browse available products or connect as a seller/rider

Placing Orders

  • Browse products from local sellers
  • Add items to your cart and proceed to checkout
  • Select your delivery address and preferred time
  • Complete payment using M-PESA or other methods
  • Track your order in real-time

Payment Issues

If you're experiencing payment problems:

  • Ensure you have sufficient funds in your M-PESA account
  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify your payment details are correct
  • Contact customer support if issues persist

Delivery Tracking

Track your delivery in real-time through our platform. You'll receive updates when:

  • Your order is confirmed
  • A rider is assigned
  • The rider picks up your order
  • The rider is approaching your location
  • Your order is delivered

IPATE Deliveries collects information to provide better services to our users. We collect information in the following ways:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, delivery addresses

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device type for security and optimization

  • Process and deliver your orders efficiently
  • Provide real-time tracking and delivery updates

  • Improve our services and develop new features

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption, secure servers, and regular security assessments.

You have the right to:

  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and deliver personalized content. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy
